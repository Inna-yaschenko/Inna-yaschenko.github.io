This is me :-)

My name is Inna. I’m the girl who brings art and technology into your everyday life, making it better, longer and easier to enjoy. I like to think that future is happening right now and I'm helping it to get closer as much as possible. I love crafting interactive experiences. Or any good experience, solved by great design. Be it an app, a website or some tangible object. Coming up with ideas to help people solve their challenges is what drives me. .
