ABOUT
Hi, I'm the UX/UI designer you are looking for. ✋
II'm the designer who brings art and technology into your everyday life - making it better, (clearer/more enjoyable), and easier to navigate. I love to craft and appreciate well-designed digital experiences. I wish to create positive experiences that are designed well.
Whether it's an app, a website, or a tangible object, using my skills to help people solve their challenges is what drives me." ✨

I graduated in 2018 with a Masters in 'Innovative Management'. When I realized my love for design, I took a course in "Design Interface". I started my design career at a marketing agency and loved it. After that, I worked at an IT company called RiverSoft as a UX/UI Designer. Now I have two years of experience and would like to continue my career in creative problem-solving.
I was first drawn to design because it is exciting and rewarding to learn about people's goals and desires, and then help them solve their problems and make their lives easier. ⭐

I enjoy the design process and love getting to know people and problem-solving. I firmly believe we can use design to make the world a better place. I'm looking forward to contributing to others' positive experiences. ❤️
I believe "the best kind of job is a paid hobby."
