EVENTSSION - A GUIDE TO EVENTS
Eventssion is a Ukraine-based startup, launched by Riversoft, that seeks to be the market leader in event management and online ticketing.
Eventssion allows users to browse, create, and promote events. Electronic tickets are sent to your account for easy access.
There are interest groups with chat rooms for members to find others with similar interests.
WEBSITE
You can view website here.
Redesigned the website in use since 2015. The focus was to create a more user-friendly design, allowing find events, buy tickets, build schedules and chat with people. Also, a great opportunity for event organizers to sell tickets, promote events, and contact event participants.
I created a persona based on user research and testimonials. This helped me communicate and present my findings with my team. The persona also ensured that users' goals and frustrations were still my focus as I progressed with the design.
Design System became a popular tool for product consistency and scalability improvements. It's a visual language and library of atomic components with clear standards that outline how the components should be used. With design system, product development team reduces repetitive work, increases productivity.
After gathering requirements, I built the entire structure of the product as a mind map. UX Architecture is a key part of product documentation to keep the product team in sync, and to communicate ideas and understanding faster. This also helps to quickly onboard new teammates. I supported this mind map throughout the entire design process and kept it updated after new features were implemented.
I created a mid-fidelity prototype of the desktop site in Figma.
I created a UI requirements document to outline all of the features and elements I wanted to incorporate into the design.
There was user testing with six users before the website was released. After every test and feedback received, I made appropriate changes to improve the product and experience.
After that, it was easier to purchase tickets, the conversion rate was higher, more tickets were sold, and the user interface was a lot friendlier.
The website was completely redesigned after this process.
